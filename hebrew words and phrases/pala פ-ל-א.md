# pala פ-ל-א

## definition

- to separate
- to distinguish
thus,
- to be singled out
- to be great
- to be extraordinary
- to be wonderful
- to astound **\[hif'il\]**
- to amaze **\[hif'il\]**
- to make distinguished **\[hif'il\]**
- to make extraordinary **\[hif'il\]**
- to make wonderful **\[hif'il\]**
