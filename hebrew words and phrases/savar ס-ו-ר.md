# savar ס-ו-ר

## definition

- to turn aside **\[pa'al\]**
- to go away **\[pa'al\]**
- to depart **\[pa'al\]**
- to remove (to cause to depart) **\[hif'il\]**

## discourse

### lehasir להסיר

	hif'il infinitive construct

### yaser יסר

	hif'il jussive third person masculine singular
